# Agent Policy

- Keep diffs small and scoped to the active task.
- Do not expand scope to adjacent features.
- Do not make automatic commits.
- Do not add secrets, credentials, or `.env` files.
- Do not install dependencies without a clear reason and human approval when needed.
- Do not run destructive commands without human approval.
- Run tests or checks required by the task.
- Require review before commit or rollback.
