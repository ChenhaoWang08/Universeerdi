# SCENARIO-0004: Controlled Demo Overlay Flags

## Goal

Verify that controlled demo trails and labels are visual overlays behind feature flags and do not change physics behavior.

## Steps

1. Launch the viewer.
2. Confirm controlled demo bodies still move under physics updates.
3. Confirm trails are visible when enabled.
4. Confirm labels are visible when enabled.
5. Confirm camera drag and zoom still work.

## Expected Result

- demo motion remains physics-driven
- trails and labels appear as optional overlays
- overlays do not imply real solar-system stability
- viewer controls remain functional
